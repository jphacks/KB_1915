To be worst , The lowest and most dejected thing of fortune , Stands still in esperance , lives not in fear : The lamentable change is from the best ; The worst returns to laughter . Welcome , then , Thou unsubstantial air that l embrace ! The wretch that thou hast blown unto the worst Owes nothing to thy blasts . But who comes here ?
